Airports

St. Augustine is accessible from several major airports. The closest is Jacksonville International Airport (JAX), about 1 hour away. Daytona Beach International Airport (DAB) is about 1.5 hours, and Orlando International Airport (MCO) is roughly 2 hours away. All three offer rental cars and rideshare options for a scenic drive into town.
